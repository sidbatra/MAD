Can I use MAD services at home?

MAD's aim is to enhance employee welfare and care in corporate world and hence we only cater to corporate segment, however if you want to host a spa party of 25 people or above don't hesitate to call us or drop an enquiry.


Will a MAD workshop disturb office environment and work place discipline?

Not at all, we work in one part of your office like any other department, without disrupting the workflow.


How much space is needed?

Any space of 5 ft x 5 ft is needed for our setup, be it your cafeteria / boardroom / lounge / lobby / medical room / recreation room. You just give us the space and we convert it into a super luxurious spa!


Do employees need to change their clothes?

Not required. On chair massages are dry therapies, wherein no oils and or lotions would be used, whereas for foot and hand therapies, just rolling up the sleeves or trousers would be enough.


Do therapies involve usage of oils / lotions?

Only for feet and hand therapies. Our therapist will ask you for preference between absorbent oils or lotions.


What hygiene standards should I expect?

Hygiene and cleanliness is given utmost importance. Before each therapy, our therapists sanitize their hands and fresh set of towels / water is used.
